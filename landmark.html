UI/UX Case Study

landmark

A hotel booking app that combines the historical with the modern. Modern UX, historic context.

The objective of this project was to design and prototype a user interface that would allow consumers to find and book a hotel without all the extra baggage from most booking experiences. The process included user research, wireframing, comping and prototyping.

user personas

Brad and Kate take small vacations multiple times a year. One of their favorite places to visit is NYC. They’ll often visit to see a show, but always make sure to stay in a historical hotel to get the full “NYC experience.” Most booking apps they use are bloated with unnecessary features that overcomplicate the process. They want a streamlined app catered to their needs.

Gary lives and works in NYC. He’s a realtor who consistently has clients fly in for property showings. He makes it a point to impress his clients by booking them rooms in historical NYC hotels. Sometimes these meetings can be last minute and Gary needs an app that will make the booking process less stressful and curated to his individual client’s tastes.
